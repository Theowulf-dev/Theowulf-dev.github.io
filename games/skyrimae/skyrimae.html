Skyrim AE Modding Hub

Tutorials

Tutorials are meant for new modders. These are structured courses I've created to teach everything from scratch, step by step. The goal is to provide a clear and organized path for learning how to work with game modding tools and concepts.

There are full scale lessons just like Lokir's Tomb, which is a full tutorial showing how to build a dungeon from start to finish.

Guides

Guides are standalone resources. These are meant to show specific pieces of information or solutions perfect for quickly checking or refreshing knowledge on a single topic.

There are short lessons just like Adding Water, which is a focused guide for working with water in a mod.
